FAQ

Short answers. No marketing fog.

Is this coaching?

No. SaverIoFlow is a system for planning, execution, and progress tracking.

Can I use it only for daily plans?

Yes. Daily planning works standalone. Goals and long-term layers are optional.

How do recommendations work?

They are small, optional suggestions based on your schedule and progress — never pushy.

What is forecasting?

An ETA plus the daily effort needed — based on your available time and estimated workload.

When should I upgrade?

After 3–5 days — when weekly planning, blocks, or forecasting becomes useful.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Simple and transparent.
